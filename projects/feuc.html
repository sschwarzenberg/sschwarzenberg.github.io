FEUC

"Website for UC student federation"

I was hired to build and design the UC Student Federation’s website. For this I had to study and set the different sections the site should have, set a graphic line, and make the illustrations used on the site.

The site was divided into different sections, each with subpages. These were:

(a) Information about FEUC: History, current board and committees, and old boards with their legacies.

(b) Treasury and spendings: All the financial information of the federation with different transparency documents.

(c) Council: Information about the political council and executive council. These pages contained the members, the main political parties, and meeting documents and transcripts.

(d) Scholarships and benefits: Detailed sections with different benefits offered to students on campus.

(f) Mosaico: The federation’s magazine and blog

(e) FAQ

(g) Contact page
